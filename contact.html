Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a rafting trip?

You can book a rafting trip by filling out the contact form above or calling us directly at (+256) 763 652 967. Our team will assist you in selecting the perfect trip for your skill level and preferences.

What is the minimum age for rafting?

The minimum age for rafting varies depending on the trip difficulty level. Most of our beginner trips are suitable for ages 8 and above. Please contact us for specific age requirements for each trip.

What should I bring?

We provide life jackets, helmets, and paddles. We recommend bringing a swimsuit, towel, water shoes, sunscreen, and a change of clothes. Avoid bringing valuable items as they may get wet.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more people. Please contact us for more information about group packages and pricing.

What if the weather is bad?

We prioritize safety above all else. In case of severe weather, we will either reschedule your trip or offer a full refund. Please check our cancellation policy for more details.

Are there any restrictions?

Participants should be in good health and able to swim. Pregnant women and people with certain medical conditions should consult with a physician before participating. Please contact us if you have specific concerns.
