Safety is Our Priority

At Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting, safety is our top priority. All our guides are fully trained and certified in:

  • Water Safety and Rescue Techniques
  • River Navigation and Assessment
  • Equipment Maintenance and Inspection

We conduct regular safety drills and equipment checks to ensure the highest standards of safety on every trip. Before each adventure, participants receive a comprehensive safety briefing covering important guidelines and emergency procedures.
