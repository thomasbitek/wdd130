Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting

Welcome to Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting — where safety, adventure, and stewardship of the river come first. Founded in 2024 by Wokorach Thomas, our mission is to create unforgettable rafting experiences while protecting the natural beauty of the river. Our core values are Safety, Adventure, Environmental Sustainability, and Customer Satisfaction.

History

Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting was founded in 2024 by Wokorach Thomas. With a passion for adventure and a love for nature, Thomas started this company to provide thrilling yet safe rafting experiences. Our team of expert guides and commitment to sustainability have helped the company grow steadily. We continue to offer a variety of trips suited to all skill levels.

Adventure Awaits You!

Adventure awaits at Bitmoi. Explore our trips and discover river sections suited to beginners through experienced rafters.
