About | Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting

Welcome to Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting, your premier destination for thrilling rafting adventures on the river. We are dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences in nature while ensuring the safety and enjoyment of our guests.

At Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting, we are passionate about providing unforgettable rafting experiences on the river. Our company was founded by Wokorach Thomas, also known as Thomas Bitek Bitmoi, who has a deep love for nature and adventure. With years of experience in the rafting industry, we have built a reputation for delivering thrilling and safe rafting adventures to our customers.

Our team of expert guides is dedicated to ensuring that every trip is not only exciting but also safe. We take pride in our commitment to environmental sustainability and strive to minimize our impact on the natural surroundings while providing an exhilarating experience for our guests.

Our History

Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting was founded in 2024 by Wokorach Thomas, also known as Thomas Bitek Bitmoi. With a passion for adventure and a love for nature, Thomas set out to create a company that would provide thrilling rafting experiences while prioritizing safety and environmental sustainability. Over the years, Bitmoi WhiteWater Rafting has grown into a reputable rafting company, known for delivering unforgettable adventures on the river. We have expanded our team of expert guides and continue to offer a variety of trips suitable for all skill levels.
